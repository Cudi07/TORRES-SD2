Eve

Japanese Vocaloid Singer

Eve (いぶ) is a popular Japanese singer-songwriter and former utaite (cover artist) on Niconico, known for a distinctively high, gentle, and "shota-like" voice. He gained prominence by covering Vocaloid songs before releasing original music, producing hits like "Dramaturgy" and "Kaikai Kitan".

Hit Albums

His albums Otogi (2019) and Smile (2020) were major chart successes, with Kaikai Kitan / Ao no Waltz topping Oricon charts.

Style and Persona

Known for a "shota-like" or "cute" voice, he often keeps his full identity mysterious, blending indie/folk rock influences.

Major Hits

"Kaikai Kitan" (Jujutsu Kaisen), "Ao no Waltz" (Josee, the Tiger and the Fish), "Fight Song" (Chainsaw Man), and "Bokurano" (My Hero Academia).
